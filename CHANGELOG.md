## [0.1.0] - 2022-10-09
### Added

- You can wrap your dependency in `bon2.deobf` method for deobfuscate it.
- Gradle extension with configuration.
  - Force mappings.
  - Mappings provider (if you use another MC dev plugin).
- Bon2Task for use BON2 in Gradle.
